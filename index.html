Services that will help you grow.

Fundamental Analysis

Economic data and news affect the value of currencies in the foreign exchange market. It is vital to understand the fundamentals to know how the economic condition of a country will impact its currency and your trades. I will provide a weekly fundamental breakdown of the economic data and its impact on the currencies.

Technical Analysis

Price Action is one of the critical factors of technical analysis. It is the core of indicators and chart patterns, but it is also one of the hardest concepts to master in technical analysis. The strategies I will be teaching are supply and demand, multiple timeframe analysis, reading and understanding candlesticks, and Wyckoff that can be used for scalping, day-trading, swing-trading, and position-trading.

Trade Breakdowns

A complete and step-by-step breakdown of trades is provided twice a week from a technical and fundamental perspective

Trading Plan and Journaling

Trading Plan and Journaling are essential components of discipline and consistency required to be a successful trader. Every student gets a customized plan tailored to his/her needs.

Support System

Every student gets access to a 1-on-1 mentorship where the mentor works him/her to improve any aspects of trading, answer any questions or concerns, and reviews trades. Also, the student receives access to a very supportive community.
